Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 22, 2025

Buzzkill helps you spend less time in selected apps using Apple Screen Time APIs. The app is private by design. It processes your selections and usage on your device. It does not send this information to a server.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit personal data to our servers. Buzzkill has no backend and no analytics SDKs.

  • App selections and usage. Your chosen apps and usage time come from Apple Screen Time and are processed on your device to create local reminder notifications. This information does not leave your device.
  • Notification content. Notification messages are generated on the device.

System Permissions We Use

  • Screen Time (FamilyControls / DeviceActivity): lets you select apps and allows on-device monitoring of those selected apps only. Required for core features.
  • Notifications: needed to deliver local reminders.

You can change these permissions in iOS Settings at any time.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your data to third parties.

Data Retention

Buzzkill does not maintain a cloud account or store personal data on a server. Settings you configure (such as selected apps, intervals, tone, language) are stored locally on your device and can be removed by deleting the app.

Crash Reports and Diagnostics

If you use TestFlight, Apple may collect crash logs and diagnostic information and share them with the developer through Apple’s tools. These reports are managed by Apple under Apple’s privacy policy. Buzzkill does not include third-party crash or analytics frameworks.

Children’s Privacy

Buzzkill is intended for individuals who are at least 13 years old, or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction. If you believe a child provided data to us through Apple channels, contact us and we will work with Apple to address it.

Security

All processing occurs on your device. We do not operate servers that hold your data.

International Transfers

We do not transfer your data internationally because we do not transmit it off your device.

Your Rights

Your rights may vary by location. Because Buzzkill does not collect or store personal data on servers, most requests can be handled by changing permissions in iOS Settings or uninstalling the app. If you have questions, contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will appear on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
