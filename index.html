How to get started

Select apps

Choose from the apps on your phone the ones that tend to steal your time the most.

Choose frequency

Set how often Buzzkill should remind you for each app: every 5, 10, 15, 25 or 30 minutes.

Personalise as you like

Pick your tone of voice in 14 languages, set buzz-free time and choose a personal daily limit.

Get notified (a lot)

Receive frequent notifications for each selected app, and you can pause everything with one tap.

Advantages over other screen time apps

  • Apps are never blocked at the wrong moment; only notifications.

  • No gimmicks or gamification, just a fast and simple app.

  • Privacy first: all data stays on your phone.
