Features

Select and focus

Choose the apps to tame with Apple’s Screen Time picker. Buzzkill watches only those picks on your device.

Timed nudges

Pick a gentle interval—5 to 30 minutes—and get reminded while you’re in the app or when you hit today’s limit.

Personal tone

Rotate friendly prompts in your chosen language to keep you aware without feeling nagged.

Privacy at the core
  • On-device only

    All processing stays on the device. Permissions live in iOS Settings.

  • No blocking

    Buzzkill does not block apps. Start or stop reminders with one control.

  • No tracking

    No screenshots, no server logs, no analytics SDKs.
