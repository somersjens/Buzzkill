How to get started

Select apps

Pick the apps that tend to steal your time. Buzzkill scans the apps on your phone so you can choose exactly which ones you want to keep in check.

Choose frequency

For each app, you decide how often Buzzkill should tap you on the shoulder. Choose reminders every 5, 10, 15, 25, or 30 minutes. You stay in control of the rhythm, while Buzzkill keeps you conscious of how long you have been scrolling.

Personalise as you like

Choose the tone of voice you prefer in 14 languages, set buzz free times, define a personal daily limit, and pause everything with a single tap whenever you need a break.

Get notified (a lot)

Based on your settings, you will start receiving frequent notifications for each selected app, chosen from more than 20,000 uniquely crafted messages that keep feeling fresh.

Advantages over other screen time apps

  • Apps are never blocked at the wrong time, Buzzkill only sends notifications.

  • No pointless gamification, just a fast, focused and simple app that gets out of your way.

  • Privacy first: all data stays safely on your phone.
